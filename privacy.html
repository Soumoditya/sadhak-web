Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12 July 2026

Sadhak respects your privacy. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information — and the choices you have.

Information we collect

  • Account information (email, display name) when you register.
  • Profile data (gender, marriage status) you provide, used only for personalization.
  • Approximate location (city / state) for accurate Panchang calculations.
  • Basic app-usage data to improve the experience.

How we use your information

  • Personalize grooming guidance based on your gender and marriage status.
  • Calculate accurate Panchang, sunrise, sunset and Rahu Kaal for your location.
  • Send spiritual reminders and festival notifications (only if enabled).
  • Display your profile in community chat, if you choose to enable it.

Data storage & security

  • Data is stored securely with Google Firebase (Firestore & Realtime Database).
  • Profile images are stored via Cloudinary over encrypted transfer.
  • We use industry-standard encryption for all data in transit.
  • We never sell, trade, or rent your personal information to anyone.

Third-party services

  • Google Firebase — authentication, database, and analytics.
  • Cloudinary — image hosting.
  • Expo Notifications — push-notification delivery.
  • We do not use any advertising SDKs or trackers.

Location data

  • Location is used only for Panchang calculations (sunrise, sunset, Rahu Kaal).
  • We store only city and state — never precise GPS coordinates.
  • Location access is optional; the app works without it.

Your rights

  • Delete your account and all associated data anytime from Settings.
  • Use the app as a Guest without providing any personal information.
  • Disable notifications at any time.
  • Request a copy of your data by emailing us.
  • Control community visibility and direct messages from your Profile.

Children's privacy

  • Sadhak is suitable for all ages and contains only spiritual / educational content.
  • We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
  • Parents or guardians may contact us to delete a child's data.
Questions about your privacy, or want your data deleted? Email soumodityapramanik@gmail.com and we'll help.
